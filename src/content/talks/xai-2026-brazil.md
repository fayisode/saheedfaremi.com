Presented at XAI 2026 in Fortaleza, Brazil (1-3 July 2026), co-located with the
4th World Conference on eXplainable Artificial Intelligence. The talk covered the
Convolutional Variational Deep Embedding (Conv-VaDE) approach and the systematic
architecture search across cluster counts, latent dimensionality, network depth,
and channel width on the LEMON resting-state EEG dataset. Companion repo with the
full sweep code:
[microstate-architecture-search](https://github.com/fayisode/microstate-architecture-search).
See the [publication](/publications/vade-eeg-microstate-discovery) for the full
abstract and arXiv link.
