consultancy. The work ran on two strands. The first was an end-to-end
document-generation system built on large language models served through Azure
ML Studio; it cut manual document-creation time by 85% while meeting the
compliance requirements of three regions. The second was customer-data analysis
in SQL and Python, which produced operational dashboards and streamlined
transaction workflows.
