UNESCO India-Africa Hackathon 2022, Gautam Buddha University, Uttar Pradesh.
23&ndash;25 November 2022. Thirty-six hours, 100 teams, 232 Indian students and 338
students from 22 African nations.

Team **Geeks_on_Fire** (Team ID UIA106):

- Rudrakshi (India, lead)
- Ayushi Saxena (India)
- Pedro Anda Ondo Nchama (Equatorial Guinea)
- Saheed Faremi (Eswatini)
- Tony Onkgopotse Richard (Botswana)
- Frank Makeba (Zimbabwe)

The team won problem statement **AGRI12: AI-based voice-assisted contact centre for assisting farmers**.

Outcome: gold medals and a team prize of ₹3 lakh.

for the full narrative.
