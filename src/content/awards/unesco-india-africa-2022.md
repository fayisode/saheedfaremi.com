UNESCO India-Africa Hackathon 2022, Gautam Buddha University, Uttar Pradesh —
November 23–25, 2022. Thirty-six hours, 100 teams, 232 Indian students plus 338
students from 22 African nations.

Team **Geeks_on_Fire** (Team ID UIA106) — Saheed Faremi (Eswatini), Rudrakshi
(India, lead), Ayushi Saxena (India), Pedro Anda Ondo Nchama (Equatorial Guinea),
Tony Onkgopotse Richard (Botswana), Frank Makeba (Zimbabwe) — won problem statement
**AGRI12: AI-based voice-assisted contact centre for assisting farmers**.

Outcome: gold medals and a team prize of ₹3 lakh (~US$3,500).

See [the team writeup](https://medium.com/@sonirudrakshi99/unesco-india-africa-hackathon-2022-2ec88cea6eb9)
for the full narrative.
